UK & Ireland Touring Programmes

Typical UK & Ireland Touring Routes

Many inbound travel programmes follow established touring routes linking major gateway airports, historic cities and regional destinations across the United Kingdom and Ireland. UK Inbound Ground Transport supports tour operators and destination management companies delivering organised travel itineraries along these routes.

London – Bath – Oxford – Cotswolds

One of the most popular touring routes for inbound visitors connects London with the historic cities of Oxford and Bath before continuing through the villages of the Cotswolds. This route is commonly used in cultural travel programmes and short touring itineraries for international groups visiting southern England.

  • London
  • Oxford
  • Cotswolds villages
  • Bath

London – York – Lake District – Scotland

This classic northbound touring route links England’s historic cities with the landscapes of northern England and Scotland. Many multi-day touring programmes travel from London through York before continuing to the Lake District and Edinburgh.

  • London
  • Cambridge or York
  • Lake District
  • Edinburgh

England – Scotland – Ireland Touring Route

Extended travel itineraries often combine England, Scotland and Ireland in a single touring programme. These routes typically begin in London before continuing to Scotland and Ireland using ferry or air connections.

  • London
  • Edinburgh
  • Glasgow
  • Belfast
  • Dublin

Scotland Highlands Touring Route

Scotland touring programmes frequently follow routes through the Highlands, connecting Edinburgh with Inverness, Loch Ness and the Isle of Skye. These journeys showcase Scotland’s dramatic landscapes and historic castles.

  • Edinburgh
  • Inverness
  • Loch Ness
  • Isle of Skye

Gateway Airport Touring Departures

Many organised travel itineraries begin or end at major UK and Ireland gateway airports. Transport planning is often structured around these arrival points to support smooth programme delivery.

  • Heathrow Airport
  • Gatwick Airport
  • Manchester Airport
  • Edinburgh Airport
  • Dublin Airport
